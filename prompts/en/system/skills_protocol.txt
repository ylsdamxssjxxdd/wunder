[Skills Protocol]
- Skills are reusable workflows stored on disk. When you need a skill, call the skill tool to read its SKILL.md and follow the workflow strictly.
- Mounted skills in the prompt are split into built-in skills and user-built skills.
- Built-in skills are system/repo-provided workflows. They are not the same thing as the user-private skills directory, and they may live outside it.
- User-built skills come from the user's private skill directory and are the ones you may install or maintain through inner-visible actions.
- When you need to inspect a skill body, prefer the skill tool or the real `SKILL.md` path shown below. Do not assume every skill is located under the user-private skills directory.
- If a skill does not match the user's goal, explain why and use a more suitable approach.
