# 中级晋升规则机读版（V2026-07）

以下规则块用于智能体检索和结构化推理。  
所有规则默认 `effective_from=2026-01-01`、`effective_to=2026-12-31`。

```yaml
- rule_id: PROM-MID-HARD-001
  type: hard
  title: 在职且正式用工
  expr: "employees.status='在职' AND employees.employment_type='正式'"
  evidence_fields: [status, employment_type]

- rule_id: PROM-MID-HARD-002
  type: hard
  title: 初级任职满24个月
  expr: "employees.current_level='初级' AND TIMESTAMPDIFF(MONTH, employees.current_level_start_date, '2026-07-31') >= 24"
  evidence_fields: [current_level, current_level_start_date]
  exception_rule: PROM-MID-EXC-001

- rule_id: PROM-MID-HARD-003
  type: hard
  title: 最近四季度绩效达标
  expr: "quarters in [2025Q3,2025Q4,2026Q1,2026Q2] AND all(grade in [S,A,B]) AND count(grade in [S,A])>=2"
  evidence_fields: [performance_quarterly.quarter, performance_quarterly.grade, performance_quarterly.score]

- rule_id: PROM-MID-HARD-004
  type: hard
  title: 2025年通过培训学时>=40
  expr: "SUM(training_records.hours WHERE training_year=2025 AND passed=1) >= 40"
  evidence_fields: [training_year, hours, passed]
  exception_rule: PROM-MID-EXC-002

- rule_id: PROM-MID-HARD-005
  type: hard
  title: 近12个月无记过及以上有效处分
  expr: "NOT EXISTS discipline_records(action_level in ['记过','严重违纪'] AND is_revoked=0 AND event_date between '2025-08-01' and '2026-07-31')"
  evidence_fields: [event_date, action_level, is_revoked]

- rule_id: PROM-MID-HARD-006
  type: hard
  title: 持有有效必需认证
  expr: "EXISTS certifications(is_required_for_promotion=1 AND valid_until>='2026-07-31')"
  evidence_fields: [cert_code, is_required_for_promotion, valid_until]
  exception_rule: PROM-MID-EXC-003
```

```yaml
- rule_id: PROM-MID-EXC-001
  type: exception
  title: 任职时长豁免
  expr: "EXISTS promotion_exceptions(rule_id='PROM-MID-EXC-001' AND approved=1 AND approved_at<='2026-07-31')"
  override: PROM-MID-HARD-002

- rule_id: PROM-MID-EXC-002
  type: exception
  title: 培训学时豁免
  expr: "EXISTS promotion_exceptions(rule_id='PROM-MID-EXC-002' AND approved=1 AND approved_at<='2026-07-31')"
  override: PROM-MID-HARD-004

- rule_id: PROM-MID-EXC-003
  type: exception
  title: 必需认证豁免
  expr: "EXISTS promotion_exceptions(rule_id='PROM-MID-EXC-003' AND approved=1 AND approved_at<='2026-07-31')"
  override: PROM-MID-HARD-006
```

- rule_id: PROM-MID-SCORE-001

  expr: "avg_performance_score*0.45 + competency_score*0.25 + potential_score*0.15 + integrity_score*0.15 >= 78"
